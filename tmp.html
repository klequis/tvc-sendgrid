This is the preheader module. It cannot be removed or moved. The first text in the preheader will be rendered by many email clients as a preview snippet of content. As a result, this text can be very critical to open rates.
You may also want to use your preheader to include a View it in your browser link.

Text Module

This is a text module, it can be used to add your marketing message. You'll find a wealth of controls for styling, formatting, and linking at the top of the editor. The text module (like most modules) can be moved by clicking and dragging the small blue icon in the top left corner of the module. Most modules can also be removed by clicking the small red icon in the top right corner of the module.
 
Module settings can be found to the left of the editor and can be collapsed and expanded as needed. Clicking on a module in the content section will open the corresponding settings menu to the left. Similarly, clicking on the settings menu on the left will highlight the corresponding drag & drop module in the content section.
